Cheesecake

Last weekend I had a cheesecake at a friend’s house and my mum made it. It was delicious. Here are the ingredients: 300 g wheat meal biscuits 500 g cream cheese 200 g castor sugar 1 tablespoon of corn flower 3 eggs 2 tablespoon of lemon juice a few drops of pure vanilla and pinch of salt 2 cups of sour cream For the whole recipe go to ”The Cook’s Companion” by Stephanie Alexander.
